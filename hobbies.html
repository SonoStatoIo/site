01 coding / videogames

Technology has always been at the center of my hobbies—from video games, which I see as a blend of art, storytelling, and innovation, to hands-on tinkering with hardware. My passion for gaming naturally evolved into an interest in coding, electronics, and building custom PCs. I enjoy exploring how things work, whether it's optimizing a workstation, experimenting with emulators and scripts, or automating my home with smart devices and Raspberry Pi. For me, every project is an opportunity to learn, have fun, and stay connected to the latest trends in tech.

02 networks / home automation

I’m passionate about building fast and secure networks, both for work and at home. My Unifi-based setup is the backbone of my smart home, where I experiment with IoT devices, automation, and custom integrations to make everyday life simpler and smarter. Whether it’s optimizing Wi-Fi coverage, integrating new IoT gadgets, or setting up a secure home lab, I enjoy turning my living space into a connected, intelligent environment.

03 raspberry pi

I enjoy trying my hand on some project with Raspberry: from PiHole to RetroPie, from OpenMediaVault to create bots and server utilities with Python. Home automation, DNS, backups, and retro gaming are just a few of my favorite uses.

04 diy

DIY is more than a hobby for me—it’s a way to learn, experiment, and bring ideas to life. I enjoy working with wood, metal, and electronics, building custom furniture, improving my workspace, and solving everyday problems with practical solutions. Over time, I’ve created everything from home office desks to smart storage systems, always looking for new challenges and ways to improve my skills. For me, hands-on projects are both relaxing and rewarding, combining creativity with technical know-how.
